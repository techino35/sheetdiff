# Privacy Policy for SheetDiff

**Last updated: 2025-08-01**

## Overview

SheetDiff ("the Add-on") is a Google Sheets add-on that compares spreadsheet data and highlights differences. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the Add-on.

---

## Information We Access

### Spreadsheet Data
The Add-on reads the content of the Google Sheets spreadsheets you explicitly select for comparison. This data is processed **entirely within your Google account** using Google Apps Script. It is never transmitted to any external server operated by the developer.

### User Properties
The Add-on stores the following data locally in your Google user properties (accessible only by you):

| Key | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| `SHEETDIFF_LICENSE_KEY` | Stores your Pro license key to enable unlimited-row processing |

No other data is stored outside of your spreadsheet.

### Google Account Information
The Add-on requests your email address (`userinfo.email` scope) solely to associate license validation with your Google account. This information is not transmitted to any third-party service.

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any spreadsheet content to external servers.
- We do **not** track usage analytics.
- We do **not** share any data with third parties.
- We do **not** use cookies or persistent identifiers beyond Google's own OAuth tokens.

---

## Data Retention

- License keys stored in User Properties remain until you manually remove them via **SheetDiff > Register Pro License > Revoke**, or until you uninstall the Add-on.
- Snapshot sheets created by the Add-on are stored within your own Google Spreadsheet and can be deleted at any time.

---

## Third-Party Services

SheetDiff does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services.

---

## Children's Privacy

SheetDiff is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Add-on after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
