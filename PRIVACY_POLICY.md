# Privacy Policy for SheetDiff

**Last updated: 2025-08-01**

SheetDiff processes spreadsheet data entirely within your Google account using Google Apps Script. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Data Stored

| Key | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| `SHEETDIFF_LICENSE_KEY` | Stores your Pro license key |
